The Future of Work is Here

FutureWorks is a group of mad scientists devoted to changing the way you work. By using advanced techniques that are too complicated to explain, this group of mathematicians, scientists, engineers, and interdimensional wizards has pioneered into a new field of science we call extrocephology, the study of brains outside of brains. With developments in our field, we have created the technology to pass information from one brain to another.

Try Our New Brain Hive Mode

The Brain Hive mode brings the most revolutionary idea-sharing platform to your brain. Never be misinformed again! Brain Hive mode gives you the ability to call upon the ideas of any other person in the network. Brain Hive mode is able to answer any question you have by simply thinking it as long as it is answerable by humankind. This method of information sharing allows everybody in the Hive to have a standard common understanding, this helps motivate every individual to do anything they want with their lives to improve the future of humankind, which helps the next generations to come with even more information to call upon.
